This policy aims to inform users about how their data is utilized within Dungeons and Dragons: Gems.

This App is intended for users 18 years of old and above. By downloading and using this App, you confirm that you are at least 18 years old

Data We Collect

We collect the following types of data from you to provide you with a personalized, useful, and efficient experience.
Generally, we may collect Non-Personal Identification from our users, including:
Device Data : Information about your device, such as its operating system, model, and unique identifiers. This data helps us ensure the App functions properly on your device.
Coarse Location: Your approximate location may be collected to provide you with location-based features or advertising.
Post Notifications: We may request permission to post notifications to keep you informed about updates, events, and promotions.
AD_ID: The advertising ID provided by your device may be collected. This identifier is unique to your device and is used for purposes such as personalizing advertising within the App, even if ads are not currently displayed. It also aids in analytics, attribution, and enhancing the overall App experience.
Network State and Internet: Information about your network connection and internet usage is collected to improve the App's performance and troubleshooting.
AppsFlyer Data: Custom in-app events, such as agreeing/disagreeing to the policy, opening settings, main menu interactions, play, quit, etc. Install and uninstall events are collected by AppsFlyer by default. This non-personal identification data is shared with AppsFlyer for analytics and attribution purposes.
Gameplay Data: We may collect data about your gameplay, including scores, points earned, and game progress. This non-personal identification data is used to personalize your experience and improve the App.
Data Analytics: Information about how you interact with the App, such as which features you use, how often you use them, and any errors or crashes you experience. This non-personal identification data is shared with AppsFlyer for analytics and attribution purposes.
Application Functionalities, Features, and Services: Various non-personal identification data is collected to enhance the functionalities, features, and services offered by the App.
Rest assured that while we generally do not share personal identification data with third-party apps, we make an exception for AppsFlyer, with whom we share non-personal identification data for analytics and attribution purposes only.
